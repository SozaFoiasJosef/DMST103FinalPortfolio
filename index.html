DMST 103 Final Portfolio - Litter

Josef Soza-Foias

I chose the topic of litter to highlight the deepening problem we have in our community. Trash is everywhere and it's a problem. Everyday we interact with garbage. Garbage that should end up in a trashcan and not on the side of a street. It’s a simple task yet if you walk across the University of Rochester campus or the Rochester city you are bound to find multiple pieces of litter. Trash cans are everywhere, there is no excuse. It may be a small inconvenience but it adds up and goes a long way. Litter is a big issue that needs attention and I hope my project can provide that recognition.

Digital Photography

My photo series highlights everyday litter that can be found. Sometimes the litter is literally in the eyesight of a trashcan. It is disappointing to find, throw it away.
